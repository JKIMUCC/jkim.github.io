Link
These two goals are linked because to have a sustainable community, it is ideal to have an affordable, clean, and renewable source of energy. Also, because we want to have all communities around the world, we will have to find sources of energy that will work all around the world. This means that to reach UN Goal 11, we will have to reach UN Goal 7 in order to make the communities sustainable.
Possible Solutions
- Find a way to make the solar cells 100% efficient.

- Gradually undergo rennovation of all buildings in a community to make them more ideal for sustainability and make it easier to utilise renewable energies.

- Find a way to be able to grow green plants on buildings, or in dry countries.
